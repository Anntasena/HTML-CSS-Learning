What Are The 5 Love Language?

Words of Affrirmation

How to communicate

  1. encorage
  2. affirm
  3. appriciate
  4. empathize
  5. listen actively

Action to take

send an unexpected note, text, or card. genuinely encourage, and often.

Physical Touch

How to communicate

non verbal - use body language and touch to express love

Action to take

  1. hug
  2. kiss
  3. hold hands
  4. slow physical affection often
  5. make intimacy a thoughful priority

Receving Gifts

How to communicate

  1. Thoughfulness
  2. make your spouse a priority
  3. speak purpousefully

Action to take

  1. Give thoughful gifts and gestures
  2. Small things matter in a big way
  3. express fratitude when receiving a gift

Quality Time

How to communicate

  1. uninterrupted and focused conversation
  2. one-on-one time is critical

Action to take

Create special moments together, take walks and do small things with your partner. weekend gateways are huge

Act of Service

How to communicate

Use action phreases like "i'll help...". they want know you're with them, partnered with them.

Action to take

Do choures together or make them breakfast in bed. Go out of your way to help alleviate their daily workload
